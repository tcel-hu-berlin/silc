Particle-Level Electrochemistry

Animation A: Coating copper with silver

Überblick:

  • The first video shows the reaction between a coiled copper wire and a solution of silver nitrate (AgNO3).
  • The animation under the video is a representation of the processes occuring at the particle-level (submicroscopic level) between the metal and the molecules in solution.
  • The animation can be started and paused with the PLAY and PAUSE buttons. The animation speed can also be controlled with a slider.
  • The animation can be displayed with or without the surrounding water molecules.
