Ion Chart

Test your knowledge of salt nomenclature! Enter the formulas and names for the various combinations of cations (rows) and anions (columns). You can hover over each ion to view its common name. Use underscore (_) to include a subscript or a "carrot" (^) to include a superscript. Press Enter to check your answer.
